Finals Journey

My Finals was a chaotic ride, no doubt about it; there were just so many things to remember due to the diverse topics we covered. It wasn't just tough for us students; even Dr. Raga seemed to have his hands full sometimes, though we probably made it worse for him, lol.

The most exciting part of it all was the building of chatbots, though, amidst all the chaos. We dived into using pre-trained models from Hugging Face, which was both fascinating and incredibly educational. Among the models we tested, the Qwen Qwen 72B parameters stood out. That model was an absolute beast! It was so powerful and efficient in processing natural language, delivering surprisingly accurate and nuanced responses.

Another significant milestone was continuing our research proposal, which focused on creating a workflow that transformed PDFs into summarized content and flashcards using various AI models. We worked with T5-large, DistilBERT, BERT, Facebook CNN, and others. Testing these models was very gratifying and intense at times, almost cinematic, especially when some of them came out better than our expectations, producing results surprisingly accurate and efficient. Meanwhile, the process was hard and each model had its learning curve and challenges in how to optimize it.

For the exams—oh what a rollercoaster ride! That piece of paper was an absolute disaster zone, but thank God all efforts were not wasted!. I saw that all the topics reviewed were present, which made it a relief. Though tough, no doubt, it challenged me to use all that I had learnt in the most challenging yet rewarding manner. Looking back now, it was an experience testing not only my knowledge but also my resilience.

This journey wasn't just about tackling difficult topics or wrestling with AI models—it was about growth. It gave me a profound appreciation for the cutting-edge advancements in AI and how they can shape the future. From building chatbots to exploring innovative workflows for information processing, this finals season was a blend of chaos, discovery, and personal achievement. It was definitely a highlight of my academic journey!
